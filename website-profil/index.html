Description

Mount Rinjani, also known as Gunung Rinjani, is an active volcano located on the island of Lombok in Indonesia. It is the second-highest volcano in Indonesia, standing at an impressive 3,726 meters (12,224 feet) above sea level. Mount Rinjani is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, lush landscapes, and challenging hiking trails.
